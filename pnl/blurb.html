GRA position for US citizen/resident

A GRA position for current OSU graduate students is available in CSE's Perception and Neurodynamics Laboratory. The position comes from a collaborative project with the Air Force Research Laboratory (AFRL) in Dayton, Ohio, which tests human speech intelligibility in multi-talker environments and models underlying perceptual mechanisms. The position holder needs to spend a day per week in AFRL, and as a result the position is open to only US citizens or permanent residents. The qualified student should hold a BS degree in computer science and engineering or a related field. Ideally the student should have a real interest in machine perception. If you are interested, please send an email with a CV to Prof. DeLiang Wang.
