Monaural Musical Sound Separation

MMSS Audio Examples


This page demonstrates the monaural musical sound separation (MMSS) algorithm proposed by Y. Li, J. Woodruff and D.L. Wang as well as a comparison algorithm by Virtanen. For details of the proposed algorithm see:
Li Y., Woodruff J., and Wang D.L. (2009): Monaural musical sound separation based on pitch and common amplitude modulation. IEEE Transactions on Audio, Speech, and Language Processing, vol. 17, pp. 1361-1371.
