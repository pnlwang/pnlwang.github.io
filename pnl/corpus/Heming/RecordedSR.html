Recorded Dataset for Speech Super-Resolution Research

Recorded by Heming Wang, Eric Healy, and DeLiang Wang in 2023. If you make use of this dataset, please quote the following paper:

  • Wang H., Healy E.W., and Wang D.L. (2024): Combined generative and predictive modeling for speech super-resolution. arXiv:2401.14269.

Description

The dataset includes carefully aligned speech recordings at multiple native sampling rates, addressing the limitations of simulated speech signals at different sampling rates. Recordings were conducted in an anechoic chamber, utilizing a HyperX SoloCast microphone, M-Audio Mobile Pre DAC, and Mackie HR824 loudspeaker. The dataset is publicly accessible in one ZIP file, provided to facilitate speech super-resolution research.
