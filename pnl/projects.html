Active Projects/Interests

    • Audition
      • Pitch and location perception
      • Motion perception
      • Speech separation
      • Speech and speaker secognition
      • Music signal analysis
      • Auditory scene analysis
    • Vision
      • Visual Scene Analysis
    • Learning
      • Deep learning for computational audition
      • Temporal sequence learning

  • Theoretical Analysis
    • Understanding neural dynamics, particularly oscillatory dynamics
